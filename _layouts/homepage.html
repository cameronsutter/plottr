Features

  • Visual Timeline

    Drag and drop your scenes, story lines, and cards to easily create a timeline of your story

  • Cards

    Each card on the timeline is like a sticky note on your wall. Add detailed descriptions about each beat of your story. Attach characters, places, or tags to make organizing your plot easy.

  • Easy to Use

    It's the easiest story timeline software you'll ever use. So many writers have said that it's the only one that works the way they think

  • Outline

    Plottr generates the outline for you from your timeline

  • Notes

    Keep all your notes together in one place. Tag and filter to keep them easily organized. Build your world, write lore, and take notes on items that are important to your story.

  • Characters

    Build a full character bio customized to your story. Sort and filter by your custom attributes.

  • Places

    Organize the places in your story with the same customization as characters.

  • Vertical or Horizontal

    Flip the timeline vertically to visualize your story in a different way. For some it's much easier to see it and build it vertically.

  • Export to Scrivener

    Take screenshots of your timeline as .png files. Export the outline and all your notes to a Word doc that is easily imported to Scrivener.

  • Markdown support

    Most descriptions support markdown syntax so you can have rich text (including embedded images)

  • Autosave

    Everything is automatically saved for you so you never lose a thing (use Dropbox, iCloud, gDrive, etc to backup your work)
